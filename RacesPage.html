Dungeons & Dragons playable 5e Races

Dragonborn

Dragon like beings that stand erect like that of a human. One of the main differennces between them and dragonss are they lack wings and a tail.
Racial Traits: +2 Strength, +1 Charisma, Dragonic Ancestry, Breath weapon, Damage Resistance

Elf

Magical people with grace, living within the world while at the same time not completely apart of it.
Racial Traits: +2 Dexterity, Darkvision, keen senses, Fey ancestry, Trance

Half-Elf

They combine what some say are the bettr qualities of their elf and human parents.
Racial Traits: +2 Charisma, +1 to two other ability scores, Darkvision, Fey Ancestry, Skill Versatility

Eladrin

Elves that reside within the feywild, a realm filled with boundless magic and peerless beauty.
Racial Traits: Elf, Darkvision, Fey ncestry, Fey Step, Keen Senses, Trance

Sea Elf

Elfs that had fallen in love with the oceans beauty and had chosen to reside within it beggining from the earliest days of the multiverse.
Racial Traits: Elf, Child of the Sea, Darkvision, Fey Ancestry, Friend of the Sea, keen Senses, Trance

Astral Elf

Elves that had ventured from the feywild to the astral plane in the desire to becoem closer to their gods. Resulting in their souls being imbued with the spark of devine light
Racial Traits: Elf, Astral Fire, Darkvision, Fey Ancestry, Keen Senses, Starlight Step, Astral Trance

Fairy

Creatures infused with magic from the feywild, most fairies look simila to small elves with incect like wings, hower each fairy has their own special physical characterisitc.
Racial Traits: Elf, DarkVision, Fey Ancestry, Fey Step, Keen Senses, Trance

Firbolg

Distant cousins of the giants, they wander the primeval forests of the multiverse where the forests entwined itself into their souls.
Racial Traits: Firbolg Magic, Hidden Step, Powerful Build, Speech of the Beast and Leaf

Dwarf

Bold and hardy, thee beings are known as skilled in their craft. From warriors to miners to crafters Dwarfs are resolute in what they do.
Racial Traits: +2 Constitution, Darkvision, Dwarven Resilience, Dwarven Combat Training, Stonecunning

Duergar

Dwarves whose ancestors had evolved from having lived for centuries within the confines of the deepest places of the Underdark.
Racial Traits: Dwarf, Darkvision, Duergar Magic, Dwarven Resilience, Psionic Fortitude

Gnome

A Gnome's energy and eenthusiasm is boundless asit poors through evry fiber of their bodies. One wonders where they get it all in their tiny bodies.
Racial Traits: +2 intelligence, Darkvision, Gnome Cunning

Deep Gnome

natives of the underdark and are covered with the subterranean realms magic. ANother name are the Svirfneblin.
Racial Traits: Gnome, Darkvision, Gift of the Svirfneblin, Gnomish Magic Resistance, Svirfneblin Camouflage

Halfling

They survive in a world far larger then themselves be avoiding all forms of notice, or otherwise any forms of offensive measures.
Racial Traits: +2 Dexterity, Lucky, Brave, Halfling Nimbleness

Orc

Due to Gruumsh's blessings they are tireless guardians and can be mighty allies wherever, even if their devotion is turned to another god.
Racial Traits: Adrenaline Rush, Darkvision, Powerful Build, Relentless Endurance

Orc 'Legacy'

They live a life with no room for weakness, where every warrior must be strong. All in order to take what is needed by force if it comes too it.
Racial Traits: +2 Strength, +1 Constitution, Darkvision, Aggressive, Primal Institution, Powerful Build

Half-Orc

Some grow to become proud leaders of Orc communities, some travle the world in an attempt to prove their value. One thing is for sure they striff to acheave something great.
Racial Traits: +2 Strength, +1 Constituton, Darkvision, Menacing, Relentless, Endurance, Savage Attakcs
