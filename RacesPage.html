Lineages

"not so much a race but a way to enhance a race being used"
Lineages are a form of ancestry that can enhance a character or do things like give unique traits. This is in a way similar to the legacy parts of races as mentioned with the different races.
Possible Lineage Buffs: +2 to an Ability Score and +1 to One Other ability Score, or +1 to Three Different Ability Scores
