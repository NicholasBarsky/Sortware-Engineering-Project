Dungeons & Dragons 5e Classes

Barbarian

A powerful warrior who can enter a fierce battle rage to destroy all that oppose them.
Main ability stat: Strength

Cleric

A devoted follower that weilds divine power in service to the divine.
Main ability stat: Wisdom

Rogue

A lurker of the shadows that relys on stealth and trickery to overcome those that get in their way.
Main stat ability: Dexterity

Druid

A magic user most attund with the forces of nature and can even shapeshift into various creatures.
Main stat ability: Wisdom

Wizard

A scholarly magic-user capable of even bending the concepts of reality itself.
Main stat ability: Intelligence

Warlock

A wielder of elderitch magic derived from contracts made with creatures from other planes of existence.
Main stat ability: Charisma

Fighter

A master of combat, skilled with a multitude of weaponry.
Main stat ability: Strength & Constitution

Ranger

A master of survival that makes use of their knowledge of the outdoors to fight.
Main stat ability: Strength & Dexterity

Monk

A master of martial arts that can harness the power of ki within themselves to accomplish the extraordinary.
Main stat ability: Strength & Dexterity

Bard

An inspiring, silver-tongued musician that can tap into the power of magic through their music.
Main stat ability: Charisma

Sorcerer

A magic user who draws their power from the magic inherited through their bloodline.
Main stat ability: Constitution & Charisma

Artificier

Masters of inventing that make use of magic and creativity to unlock and create extraordinary items.
Main stat ability: Constitution & Intelligence

Blood Hunter

Willing to go through any amount of pain and suffering to achieve their goals, these warriors forged themeselves to be able to protect the innocent.
Main stat ability: Dexterity or Strength & Intelligence or wisdom
"please note that the blood hunter class originally was made from the group critical role so technically its a homebrew that is widely known"
